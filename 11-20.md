## [Level 12](https://notpron.com/notpron/blame/crossview.htm)

From the page we are presented an image, nothing too interesting with just that so we check the source code.

In there we see two comments at the bottom:

- `<!--GIF me my picture back-->`
- `<!--http://www.deathball.net/temp/dragon.zip-->`

Opening the link it downloads the zip, we decompress it and obtain a `.wmv` video file, it shows the dragon from the image depicting the hollow face illusion and a cat but nothing that meaningful, for a moment I thought that the hint when you open the form "attention whore" meant something with the cat since some are clingy but just a hunch, so maybe they'll be useful later on.

Then the other things we see in the source is a comment just below the "I'm ruining the third dimension!" text `<!--name me-->`, this indicates that the credentials probably have something to do with an object in the image ruining the third dimension.

At first I thought the obvious with the dragon being the most notable thing in it, googling revealed that its name is "Thinky the Dragon" but no matter what combination I put in the credentials it didn't work, I even tried combining it with the cat, also looked if there was some relation to G4G (the creators of the Dragon) and some cat, there seemed to be another optical illusion by them called "Cheshire Cat" but there was no combination that worked.

Then I gave up on that idea and tried to work out something else, there was also another comment that I haven't talked about yet, `<!-- switch pics for parallel view -->`.

I wasn't sure what parallel view meant so I googled it and got another term, stereograms, and if you look at the title of the page it says stereo, so surely this must be the right way.

So then with an image editing software I tried to overlap both sides of the image, in particular I used [Photopea](https://www.photopea.com/) and since it didn't seem to overlap the whole thing together I tried with each object individually, and at first I thought that the wristwatch and tv remote were the ones ruining the 3rd dimension since both didn't seem to align at all.

After this realization I also tried with a lot of combinations for both of them but no luck, so finally I thought that the remote was obviously not aligning but the watch was mostly correct, so maybe just the remote was the right answer since the text said "I'm ruining", not "We're ruining", it also aligned with the hint "attention whore" since tvs are just that.

That must mean its just one object, so I tried tv remote but no luck, until at last I thought of alternative names for it, with that in mind I finally got the right credentials, to get the form just click either of the 12s in the image.
